O Gosto de Beirute, real and authentic lebanese food here in Barretos for the first time. There you can find the real lebanese sandwitches wrapped in a home-made bread, which recipe is very delicate and precise. It is not over yet, you can also find the original fast bites. More is coming soon!

Inside the restaurant you can find various images that hold stories for beautiful moments, historic places, and touristic beauty in Lebanon.

Enjoy! A real juicy burger brought to you all the way from the kitchens of Lebanon. Burgers like this don't grow on trees 7aw 7aw. But the menu does not stop there, you'll find Shawarmas, Fajitas, and the one and only Falafel.
